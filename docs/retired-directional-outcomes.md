plot card as its canonical back. Mechanics were rewritten to the normalized
exchange-or-addition contract. The other horizontal branch is recorded here
for possible reuse as future authored cards; a dash means the former card had
no second horizontal branch.

Effect abbreviations: `L` = Eldritch Lore, `C` = Crew, `S` = Sanity. `Add`
names the newly unlockable card. All listed entry effects are explicit in
production data; `‚Äî` means `null`.
